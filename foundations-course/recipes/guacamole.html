Guacamole

Unleash the power of the magic Avocado with an ancestral recipe from Guatemala

Get ready to have the best Guacamole on the block homie!

Ingredients

  • 3 Avocados
  • 1/2 Onion
  • 1 Lemon
  • Some Cobanero Chili, if you dare

Steps

  1. Smash those avocados
  2. Chop that onion and mix it with the avocados yo!
  3. Squeeze the lemon all over
  4. Add chili if you wanna live this life to the max
